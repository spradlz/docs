## Merge-field syntax

A-Frame templates use `$Token` merge fields, in four families:

| Family | Example | Resolves to |
|---|---|---|
| `$f_<Field>` | `$f_ContractTitle` | a custom field value (see the field tree) |
| `$d_<Date>` | `$d_ClosingDate`, `$d_ClosingDate-StartTime` | a transaction date/event |
| role-contact | `$Buyer-FirstNames`, `$Seller-LastName`, `$TitleCompany-Company`, `$Lender-FirstName`, `$UserPrimaryAgent-FullName`, `$UserAssistant1-FullName` | a participant field by role |
| property | `$PropertyFullAddress`, `$PropertyAddress`, `$ContractPrice` | the transaction's property/financials |
