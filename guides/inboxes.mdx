## The model

- **Numbers are a dumb directory** ([Numbers](/guides/phone-numbers-and-call-routing)): a number is four facts —
  what it is · its label · who carries it · **which inbox it feeds**. No behavior lives on a number.
- **The inbox owns ALL behavior.** Doors (numbers + email addresses) forward in; one unified stream comes out
  (texts, calls, email — mixed, time-ordered, the Follow Up Boss My/Company/Past-Agents pattern).
- **Two kinds, and only two:**
  - **Personal** — created automatically, one per team member (humans AND AI agents): their line + their email.
  - **Shared** — created on purpose: Company · Past Agents · Vendor Mail · (any new function, e.g. Transaction
    Coordination).
- **The ring rule** — one readable chain per inbox: ① the manager answers (if it's an AI with the Calls channel
  on) → ② ring humans, in order → ③ voicemail + recording → card on the lead. An **AI backup** is just a
  ring-chain step on a human's inbox ("Rings Ana → no answer: Myli picks up") — never a manager swap.
- **The manager** — a human team (they just work the stream) or an AI agent, **per channel** (calls / texts /
  email each have their own switch). An inbox is fully functional with zero AI.
- **The laws (not settings):** replies go out the same door they came in · caller/sender-ID matches an existing
  lead → update their record, never a dupe.
- **The prompt layer** — the inbox holds ONLY the situational layer ("callers here found us on Google…"). The
  agent's personality lives on their agent card; the voice engine + phone doctrine live on the Voice page.

## Data model (mig `0390_inboxes.sql`)

- **`hl_inboxes`** — `kind` (personal/shared) · `owner_user_id` (personal) · `manager_kind` (human/ai) +
  `manager_user_id` · `ai_channels` jsonb `{calls,texts,email}` · `ring_chain` jsonb (ordered `user` steps;
  voicemail is the implicit terminal) · `spam_mode` · `prompt_layer` · `is_active`.
- **Doors:** `hl_phone_numbers.inbox_id` (a number lives in exactly ONE inbox) + `hl_inbox_email_doors`
  (addresses, e.g. Vendor Mail = `notifications@houseloop.ai`).
- **Backfill (fail-closed):** personal inboxes auto-provisioned per active team member (AI agents self-manage
  their own identity lines); shared Company / Past Agents / Vendor Mail seeded **human-managed with every AI
  channel OFF** — no migration ever turns AI answering on. Doors routed by the old category taxonomy.
- **Deprecated in place (not dropped):** `hl_routing_groups`, `hl_phone_numbers.answer_mode` /
  `.routing_group_id` — the voice webhook still reads them until the ring-chain runtime cutover ships.

## API

- `GET /api/inboxes` · `POST /api/inboxes` (shared only) · `GET/PATCH /api/inboxes/[id]`
  (name, manager, per-channel flags — merged jsonb so one flip can't clobber another —, ring_chain, spam,
  prompt_layer, is_active). Loader: `lib/inboxes/load.ts`.
- `GET /api/inboxes/[id]/stream` — the unified feed, read-only over `hl_messages` + `hl_calls`, filtered by the
  inbox's doors (last-10 phone match / address match).
- `PUT /api/phone-numbers` accepts `inbox_id` (the directory's Feeds-inbox control).
