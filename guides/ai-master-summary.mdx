## The vision
Every AI surface and every AI configuration in **ONE first-class top-level `AI` section** ‚Äî nothing more than 1‚Äì2
clicks deep. Today AI is sprawled across 7+ nav locations and its config is buried ~5 levels deep
(Settings ‚Üí My Team ‚Üí agent ‚Üí Roles ‚Üí Capabilities), with Myli / AI Clone / AI ISA living under **My Team ‚Üí Members**
(the wrong home). Competitor **SureSend** ships the consolidated version (an AI Database Manager + an AI ISA). We're
pulling all of ours into one place.

## What already exists (why we move fast)
The **data spine is already built** ‚Äî this is mostly surfacing what's buried, not a rebuild:
- `hl_users.type='ai_agent'` (Myli, AI Clone, AI ISA) + `clone_of_user_id`
- `hl_role` ‚Äî the reusable role/shell: `persona`, `voice_persona`, `tools` (JSONB capability gates), `docs`, `model`, `kind`
- `hl_member_role` ‚Äî per-agent overrides
- `CAPABILITY_TREE` (`lib/agent-runtime/capabilities.ts`) ‚Äî 6 categories, 26 capabilities (Comms ¬∑ Search Homes ¬∑ Appointments ¬∑ Tasks ¬∑ CRM ¬∑ **Calls & Voice** = voice_inbound / capture_caller / set_callback / take_message / flag_spam)
- Leads carry `ai_agent_user_id` / `ai_role_id` / `ai_clone_id`

**Net-new (the only ground-up builds):** AI Pond, AI Inbox, AI Database Manager.

## The target information architecture ‚Äî one top-level `AI` section
- **AI Overview** (`/ai`, evolves today's `/ai-follow-up`): vertical agent **business cards** (Myli ¬∑ AI Clone ¬∑ AI
  ISA ‚Äî headshot, name, phone, email, one-click Persona / Capabilities / Connections / Assignments) + master toggles +
  performance KPIs + the AI-Services cards.
- **AI Services** (each its own first-class page): **Agent ¬∑ Voice ¬∑ Inbox ¬∑ Pond ¬∑ Database Manager**.
- **AI Roles**: reusable role/capability configs (today `hl_role` has no nav entry).
- Myli / AI Clone / AI ISA move **out of My Team ‚Üí Members** into the AI section.

### The two flavors of AI Voice
1. **Lead-agent voice** ‚Äî an AI agent on a *lead* answers ‚Üí texts back from the **lead's/agent's line**.
2. **Company-inbox voice** ‚Äî company numbers (GMB **913-320-0906**, sign, past-agent) route into an **AI Inbox** ‚Üí
   per-inbox prompt ¬∑ lead-source attributed by the dialed number ¬∑ texts back through the **company inbox line** ¬∑
   creates-lead toggle ¬∑ per-bucket spam ¬∑ caller-ID updates an existing lead instead of duping.
