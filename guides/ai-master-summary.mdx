## The locked design (2026-07-13) — three shapes + a comms layer

The whole AI answers three questions, so the section has three shapes. Every page belongs to exactly one.
**There is no "AI Overview" — there is one control room.**

1. **🎛️ AI Cockpit** (`/ai`) — *"Is it on, what's it doing, what's it costing me?"* ONE page: the master
   switches (AI Follow-Up · AI Voice), performance KPIs (Today/7d/30d), the live AI activity feed (the AI
   lens of the run log, per-action cost on every row), the Spend section (MTD by agent vs the monthly cap),
   and a Services status band that fronts the four services. The `/ai-cost` and `/run-log` deep pages
   survive and are linked from here — their nav entries die.
2. **👥 AI Agents** (`/ai/agents`) — *"Who's on my AI team, and what can they do?"* The roster of **fixed,
   identical cards** (300×356; four stat slots, always rendered, "—" when empty; one "Open Agent" button) +
   **Roles as the second tab** (the reusable shells: persona · capability gates · docs · model). Click a
   card → **Agent Detail** (`/ai/agents/[id]`): five tabs — Overview · Persona (the composed prompt, each
   layer linking to its source) · Capabilities (all 26, role-inherited, overrides highlighted) ·
   Connections · Assignments (the agent's full duty roster: leads, voice, inboxes managed + backed up,
   pond, database manager). This kills the 5-level Settings → My Team burial.
3. **🏗️ AI Services** — *"Where do the agents work?"* Each first-class, all sharing one rhythm
   (**who works it → performance → config → the work, with receipts**):
   - **🎙️ Voice** (`/ai/voice`) — the v1-LOCKED voice page re-homed: engine (model/voice/temperature,
     changes apply next call), the live-composed prompt, the call-card run log. The engine + phone doctrine
     live HERE, once, globally. "Where she answers" reads the Inboxes layer.
   - **🌊 Pond** (`/ai/pond`) — the unassigned-lead pool. Plain-English rules in two groups (*falls in
     when* / *comes out when*); the exits are always-on LAWS (shows life → routed to a human; a human
     claims it → out instantly). The pond table leads with the SIGNAL column, hottest first.
   - **🗄️ Database Manager** (`/ai/database-manager`) — the nightly auditor (a registered Inngest run).
     Every rule carries its own leash: **Fix it** (act + receipt) or **Flag it** (propose, human approves);
     merges are LOCKED to flag-only. Findings table + the 7 AM daily report.
4. **🧩 Context Blocks** stay in the AI group under a small LIBRARY zone.

### The comms untangle — Numbers → Inboxes → a manager (NOT an AI feature)

Locked with the section (Zach, same session): **inbox management is comms infrastructure and lives under
Phone & Voice (Twilio · Numbers · Inboxes)** — the AI is just one possible *manager* of an inbox.

- **Numbers = a dumb directory.** A number is four facts: what it is · its label · who carries it (+A2P) ·
  **which inbox it feeds**. Unrouted numbers surface loudly. What DIES on today's phone-numbers page: the
  "Answered by" dropdown, the "Type" taxonomy, the "Group" column, the separate FUB-numbers band, and the
  "Routing groups" section — five inventions of the same missing concept (the inbox).
- **The inbox owns ALL behavior.** Two kinds only: **personal** (auto-created, one per team member — their
  line + their email; AI agents are team members too) and **shared** (created: Company · Past Agents ·
  Vendor Mail · Transaction Coordination). Doors in → one FUB-style unified stream out (texts, calls,
  voicemails, email). Each inbox has a **ring rule** (one readable chain: ① the manager answers, if it's
  an AI with Calls on → ② ring humans in order → ③ voicemail + recording → card) and a **manager — a human
  or an AI, per channel** (Company: Myli on calls + texts + email). Plus: creates-lead toggle,
  replies-out-the-same-door (law), caller/sender-ID dedupe (law), spam bucket, and the inbox's
  *situational* prompt layer only.
- **AI backup** = a ring-chain row, not a manager swap ("Rings Ana → no answer: Myli picks up → card").
- **Where each prompt lives:** the agent's personality → its agent card. The voice engine + phone
  doctrine → the Voice page, once, globally. The situational layer → the inbox. On a call she is
  persona + doctrine + situation.
- **The scaling rule — the model never grows a new concept:** new human → personal inbox auto-exists;
  new function → create a shared inbox; new AI agent → a team member, seatable as manager or backup.

  capability gates · docs · model · kind); `hl_member_role` per-agent overrides

- Masters: `GET/PATCH /api/settings/ai-conversations` (`enabled`, `voice_enabled`)
- KPIs + feed: `/api/ai-follow-up/{stats,feed,agents,agent-cards}` over `hl_lead_activity`
  (`actor_role='ai_agent'`); AI cost = read-time tokens × model rate (`stampActivityTokens`)
- Phone spine: `hl_phone_numbers` · `hl_routing_groups` · `hl_number_ports` · `hl_phone_forward_rules` ·
  the FUB number sync · `lib/phone/resolve-subtype.ts`

**Net-new (ground-up):** the `hl_inboxes` data model (greenfield — no inbox table exists today),
AI Pond, AI Database Manager.
