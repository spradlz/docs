<Warning>
**Status: NOT BUILT (spec only).** This documents a feature that was designed in the locked wireframe and
planned across two guides, but never wired. Today, moving a lead to **Under Contract** fires the lightweight
[DealMilestoneModal](/guides/lead-detail-stages-and-deals) (address · price · commission · closing → one
`hl_lead_deals` row) — NOT the full loop intake. The native funnel exists
([AddTransactionFunnel](/guides/native-transaction-intake)) but is only reachable manually via the band's
**View / Add Transactions** hub, and only Step 1 (Contract & Dates) is wired. This page is the build spec for
closing that gap.
</Warning>

## The one idea

**Moving a lead to Under Contract IS the moment a real transaction begins — so that stage move should open the
full TC intake funnel, not a one-row deal capture.** The funnel auto-fills from the lead AND the selected loop,
captures the whole transaction (the ~140-field Cognito schema), creates/links the loop, writes the two ★ anchor
dates, and projects the Chapter-2 contract stepper. One flow, buyer or seller.

This is the "section that's supposed to pop up when you hit Under Contract."

## Where it fits — the two-chapter loop

It's the hinge between the two chapters of the [TC Loop band](/guides/native-transaction-intake):

1. **Chapter 1 — Shopping** (the Agency Loop): agreement prep → sent → signed. No transaction chrome.
2. **→ Under Contract** — the stage move fires **this funnel**.
3. **Chapter 2 — Contract** (the Property Loop): the funnel's anchor dates drive Under Contract → EMD → … → Possession.

The Under-Contract gate node in the shopping stepper ("→ Under Contract · starts loop") is the visual promise of
this trigger.

## The trigger (unbuilt)

| Today | Planned |
|---|---|
| Stage → Under Contract → **DealMilestoneModal** (5 fields → `hl_lead_deals` row) | Stage → Under Contract → **the TC intake funnel** (full intake → creates/links the **loop** + records + links the deal) |
| Fires from `PipelineChipDropdown` / `CenterLane` intercept matrix | Same intercept, but routes Under Contract to the funnel instead of the deal modal |
| Captures a deal, not a transaction; no loop, no dates, no stepper | Captures the transaction, writes the 2 anchors, projects the dated stepper |

The intercept matrix lives in [Lead Detail — Stages, Deals & Audit Trail](/guides/lead-detail-stages-and-deals)
(`Anything → Under Contract`). Wiring this feature means that row opens the funnel.

## The pre-fill (the part Zach remembers)

On open, the funnel auto-fills from **two sources**:

- **The lead** (`hl_leads`): name, phone, property address, assigned agent, lead source.
- **The selected loop** (`hl_transactions` + children): side, agency price, participants, and any agency-chapter
  data already captured. **This loop-side pre-fill is the piece that is NOT built** — today only the lead
  address/phone/agent pre-fill (`AddTransactionFunnel` `prefill` prop).

## The intake (the deep capture — unbuilt)

The funnel's Steps 2–6 are the ~140-field intake, rebuilt natively from the
[Cognito Forms schema](/guides/cognito-forms-api) (TC Intake Form Buyers #103 / Sellers #108). Today they render
as **structural placeholders** — the deep field capture was deferred to "the Transaction Summary pass" and never
built. The A-Frame `Collection → Group → Field` map (Property · EMD · HOA · Inspections · Financing · Commission)
is documented in the Cognito guide.

## On save — what it must do

1. **Create / link the loop** — `createLoop` if none for that side (seeds the side's milestones + tasks), else link.
2. **Write the two anchor dates** — `EffectiveDate` (Under Contract) + `ClosingDate` → `hl_tc_loop_event.event_date`
   via `PATCH /api/tc/loops/[id]/dates` (built), so the date engine projects the rest of the stepper.
3. **Record + link the deal** — the `hl_lead_deals` milestone row is created AND linked to the loop (one
   transaction, not two orphan rows). **The deal↔loop link is NOT built.**
4. **Write the audit row** — `deals.under_contract` to `hl_audit_log`, same as today.
