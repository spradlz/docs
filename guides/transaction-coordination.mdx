## What this section is

**Transaction Coordination (TC)** is the HouseLoop CRM section for running a deal — the coordination
layer (transactions, contracts, email queue, team files) on top of the transaction-management platform
(dotloop) that holds the loop file. Modeled as a *light*, AI-native (Myli-fillable) version of a
dedicated TC tool, built on HouseLoop's own rails.

Where [Website Integration](/guides/listing-lifecycle) is about the *listing* and the CRM guides are
about the *lead*, **Transaction Coordination is about the deal** — the contract-to-close record.

## The model

| Layer | Source of truth | HouseLoop role |
|---|---|---|
| **Listing** (the home) | `Listing` (web DB) | renders `/home/<slug>`, value, history |
| **Lead** (the person) | `hl_leads` (CRM DB) | CRM, AI follow-up, activity log |
| **Transaction** (the deal) | **`hl_transactions`** (CRM DB) | the deal record — written by disposition |

**The transaction record already exists: `hl_transactions`** (disposition-driven — marking a lead
*Under Contract* or *Closed* in the pipeline writes a row with address · price · closing · commission;
`milestone` = `under_contract` \| `closed` \| `fell_through`). **Past clients = leads with a `closed`
transaction.** The TC tables that sit alongside it: `hl_contract_template` (fillable form store),
`hl_contract_fill` (a filled contract, per-lead), `hl_tc_email` (the email queue). dotloop stays the
system of record for signed docs; HouseLoop reads loops and pushes finished documents back.

## The CRM section (navigation)

TC is a **drill-in section**: the main left nav has **Transaction Coordination ›**; clicking it swaps
the whole left menu for the TC sub-nav + a **Back to CRM** link. Sub-nav (routes under `/tc/*`,
additive — Dashboard/Leads unchanged):

**Transactions · Email Queue · Forms · Team Files · Team Info · Team Members · Lists · Client Portal · Import**

## Forms — the Contract Engine

The **Forms** page turns the brokerage's flat dotloop contract PDFs into **fillable forms** HouseLoop
can complete programmatically:

1. Each form's flat PDF + a coordinate **field-map** (PDF field → lead/deal data source) is stored in
   `hl_contract_template` (`lib/tc/contract-forms.ts` holds the maps).
2. The engine (`lib/tc/fill-contract.ts`, pdf-lib) adds AcroForm fields and fills each from the lead
   (name, search area, budget, agent, dates) — Myli-verified values override.
3. Pick a form → fill → **preview the filled PDF inline** → download → (Stage 4) push into the dotloop loop.

The **showing-door** forms (the buyer journey): **Single Property Buyer-Broker Rep** (default, one
house) → **Exclusive Buyer Agency** (multiple homes). Disclosures (wire fraud, ABA, broker disclosure)
roll in later at contract time, not at showing.
