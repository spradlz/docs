**In the app:** CRM sidebar → **Pipeline & Workflow** → **Relationships** — [`/settings/relationships`](https://crm.houseloop.ai/settings/relationships)

A **relationship** is how a contact stands with *you* — it is its own axis, separate from where the lead *came
from* (that's the lead source). It's managed with the exact same builder as [Stages](/guides/lead-detail-stages-and-deals):
drag to reorder, toggle active, edit color/icon, add/delete.

![Relationship Management — the builder, reusing Stage Management](/images/settings-relationships.png)

## The model

**The base three — every agent sees these** (system, can't be deleted):

| Relationship | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Lead** | The default — someone you're working, not yet sphere or past client. |
| **Sphere of Influence** | Friends, family, your book — people who refer, not (yet) transacted. |
| **Past Client** | Has closed a deal with you. A Sphere-of-Influence contact who converts becomes a Past Client. |

**Admin-scoped custom relationships** — visible only to admins (e.g. Ana is an admin; regular agents never see
these). Seeded for Ana's book: **Ana Past Client · Ana Past Client Handoff · SRG Team Past Client.** Admins add
their own via **+ Add Relationship** (name, color, icon, and a scope of *all agents* or *admins only*).

## Why it replaced the old buckets

The old `hl_leads.relationship_bucket` was a hard-coded enum (`ana_native · ana_only · ana_team · srg_team ·
ana_past_client · srg_past_client · dnc`) that **conflated three different things** into one dropdown: the
relationship, whose book it is, and a do-not-contact flag. It was replaced 2026-07-10:

- **Relationship** (this builder) = the client's standing. Customizable.
- **Whose book / servicing** = a separate concern (the admin-scoped customs capture Ana's book without polluting
  the base set every agent sees).
- **DNC** = a status flag, not a relationship — dropped from this axis.
- **Origin** (Zillow / Realtor / referral) stays on the **lead source**, never here — so a past client who came
  from Zillow keeps *both* truths.

## Where it shows

The relationship renders on the lead's **[Referral Dashboard band](/guides/lead-detail#referral-dashboard-band)**
(`?view=referral`) — the dropdown is populated live from this builder, scoped to the viewer's role.

## Under the hood

- Table `hl_relationships` (`key`, `name`, `color`, `icon`, `position`, `is_active`, `is_system`, `scope`) —
  migration `0363`. The lead stores the relationship's `key` in `hl_leads.relationship_bucket`.
- API `/api/relationships` (list · create · reorder) + `/api/relationships/[id]` (update · delete) — mirrors
  `/api/stages` exactly, which is why the same `StagesPage` renders it. Writes are admin-only.
