<Info>
**Status: LIVE.** The Receptionist is the first shipped AI Service — Myli answering inbound calls on the
front-door lines. Screen: **CRM → AI → Receptionist** (`/ai/receptionist`).
</Info>

# What the service is

**The job: answer the front doors.** When a covered line rings, Myli picks up — instant greeting, seller/
buyer/past-client triage, the intake funnel, and a wrap-up that lands the transcript, summary, lead, and
callback plan in the CRM. Cockpit card copy: *"Answers the front doors — uses Voice + Inboxes."*

# The screen (`/ai/receptionist`)

- **The job** — one seat, one switch. The on/off here is the SAME wire as the Cockpit's Receptionist
  toggle (`ai_conversations.voice_enabled`, fail-closed — off means no line answers).
- **Inboxes she covers** — read from the per-inbox manager seats; set the seat on each inbox and it shows
  up here.
- **The instrument** — voice is EQUIPMENT: engine, voices, prompt, and the call log are configured once on
  the [AI Voice](/guides/ai-voice) page.
