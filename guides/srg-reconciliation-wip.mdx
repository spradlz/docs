## What we've done
Harvested 5 source tables over 2 days (SISU, FUB deal report, MLS rooftop history, dotloop, FUB Haiku brains). The **s6**
agentic resolver (~$200 Sonnet) ran on all ~1,834 on the **29th** → populated the cards with deals, the **household FUB-ID
resolution** (`fub_identity`, 94% coverage, dedup + spouse merge), and live GIS/deed discoveries (current homes, lost
buyers). **13 gold cards were hand-corrected by Zach on the 28th = ground truth.**

## The fuck-up
1. Built a **from-scratch resolver** that ignored s6's discoveries and wrote to a **scrap table** the audit never reads → thin/wrong cards + constant "my list vs your page" divergence.
2. **Name-match bug:** exact first-name match dropped SISU deals filed under a partial name ("Kyle" vs "Kyle & Karly") → Nations lost 2 of 5 deals + its lost listing.
3. **Brain harvest ran on the WRONG pool** (`hl_fub_sisu_link`) → dossiered 3,542 non-clients, **skipped 1,044 past clients** incl. Nations.
4. **Dotloop licensee 85% empty** (10,361/12,143 NULL agent) — reported "done" off a process exit, never measured coverage.

## Lessons (locked)
- **The table is the truth. ONE source.** Resolver writes the SAME canonical tables the audit reads.
- **"Done" = correct data IN the table, verified by query.** Measure coverage, never trust "exit 0."
- **Reconcile, don't rebuild.** Combine s6's discoveries with the harvest tables; correct dispositions, keep GIS finds.
- **Match fuzzy (surname + first-token); merge households.** Prove on 20 vs the golds before scale.
