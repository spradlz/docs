## What this section covers

The website is our Zillow: one owned `Listing` record per home, each rendering a permanent
`/home/<canonicalSlug>` page. The CRM is our contact book. "Website Integration" is everything that keeps
those two in sync — how a home becomes a listing, how it's addressed, how its value/photos/history are
filled, how on-page engagement is tracked, and how an outside home (e.g. one a lead viewed on Zillow)
resolves back to our listing page.

## The matching contract (read this before "why didn't this home link?")

A constant question across the CRM and the website: **"this lead looked at a home on Zillow — why does it /
doesn't it link to a HouseLoop page?"** The answer is one rule with two parts.

<Steps>
  <Step title="A home can only link if WE carry it">
    Our `Listing` table is the Heartland MLS IDX feed — the **Kansas City metro**. If a lead viewed a home
    in Topeka, Lawrence, Eudora, or anywhere outside our MLS, that home is **not on HouseLoop at all** — it
    was never in our feed. There is nothing to link to, and the link correctly grays out. This is coverage,
    not a bug. A `zpid` wouldn't change it: a Topeka home has a Zillow id too, but it's still not in our table.
  </Step>
  <Step title="If we carry it, we match by MLS# first, then address">
    - **MLS number** (when the source provides one) → exact match to `hl_heartlandmls_<mls>` / `Listing.mlsId`.
    - **Otherwise address** → house number + ZIP + the distinctive street word. This is
      **suffix-abbreviation-proof** (it never compares "Ave" vs "Avenue", "Trfy" vs "Trafficway", "Rd" vs
      "Road") and **ordinal-proof** (it treats "27th" and "27" as the same). Same address = same home, always.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
**We do NOT join on Zillow's `zpid`.** Zillow ids every home with a `zpid`, but our `Listing` table stores
`mlsId`, not `zpid`. So the join key is **MLS# → else address**, never the Zillow id. Adding a `zpid` column
(backfilled from our existing Zillow enrichment) would tighten precision on edge cases — it would **not**
expand coverage, because the ceiling is "is this home in our MLS feed," not "can we match the key."
</Warning>

The canonical home for the address/identity logic is
[Property Data & Address Identity](/guides/property-data-and-address-identity). The same matcher backs two
surfaces, so they must stay identical:

- **CRM lead-activity cards** — the "View on HouseLoop" link on a home a lead browsed.
- **Website /home engagement** — seeding real views/likes/saves onto a listing's on-page counts.
