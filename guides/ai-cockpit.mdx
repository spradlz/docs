## The five instruments (top to bottom)

1. **Master Controls** — the kill switches, same wires as always (`hl_settings.ai_conversations`):
   **AI Follow-Up Engine** (`enabled`, fail-open) and **AI Voice** (`voice_enabled`, fail-closed). Off here = off everywhere.
2. **Performance** — the KPI funnel (`StatStrip`: 6 heroes + atomic sub-tiles, tap-through filters the feed)
   + "how leads ended" outcomes. Data: `/api/ai-follow-up/stats` over the canonical run-log
   (`hl_lead_activity`, `actor_role='ai_agent'`).
3. **Live AI Activity** — the feed under a `MenuTable` header (agent tabs · lens filters · timeframe).
   Data: `/api/ai-follow-up/feed`. The full [Run Log](/guides/run-log) stays the forensic timeline.
4. **Spend** — month-to-date by agent against the monthly cap. Derived at read time: the run-log's stamped
   tokens × each agent's configured model rate (`MODEL_PRICING`). New endpoint `/api/ai-follow-up/spend`
   (cap in `hl_settings.ai_spend.monthly_cap_usd`, default $250). [AI Cost](/guides/ai-cost) remains the
   billing/attribution truth — this is the glanceable number.
5. **Services** — the status band fronting the four service surfaces: **Voice** (master state) ·
   **[Inboxes](/guides/inboxes)** (live "AI manages N of M" from `hl_inboxes`) · **Pond** and
   **Database Manager** (honest "Not set up" until C5/C6 ship).
