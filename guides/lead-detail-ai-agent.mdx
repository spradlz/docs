The lead detail page follows the **two-lane parity** model: the **LEFT lane** is the **human agent +
their AI clone** (human Agent + AI Mode toggle, Collaborators, Contact, Tags); the **RIGHT lane** is
**Myli**, the assigned AI. Both lanes render the same skeleton — an always-on **📋 Tasks &
Appointments** section plus a gated **🤖 AI Workflow** section — see
[Lead Detail — Parity Lanes](/guides/lead-detail-parity-lanes).

This page documents the **RIGHT lane's AI Agent card** and the two buttons that open Myli's dossier:
**Context View** and **Brain Map**. The AI Workflow reasoning chain (Active Triggers → Current
Workflow → Current Action → Next Actions → Recent Actions) is the shared `AiReasoningChain` rendered
on **both** lanes (`actor` = `clone` on the left, `myli` on the right) — see
[Closed Loop Follow-Up](/guides/closed-loop-follow-up).

## The AI Agent card

| Element | What it is |
|---------|-----------|
| 🤖 **AI Agent dropdown** | The assigned AI agent + role (e.g. Myli · AI ISA). `assigned_ai_agent_id` — Myli by default, swappable. |
| **Context** button | Opens the **Context View** (Myli's read). Spec below. |
| **Brain Map** button | Opens the **Brain Map** (the fact constellation). Spec below. |
| **Capabilities** (own card) | The agent's role tools as permission toggles (Text, Email, Book, Search…). Mirrors the lead's Collaborators card. |
| **Contact** (own card) | The AI agent's comms identity — email · call/text · iMessage. |

**Status:** The AI Agent card, Capabilities, Contact, the 🤖 emoji treatment, and the Context /

planned build specced below (the buttons currently show "· soon"). The facts behind both come from
the Memory engine in the Myli Closed-Loop SSOT (`GT_HOUSELOOP_MYLI_CLOSED_LOOP_FOLLOWUP_v1` §5).
</Note>

## The fact taxonomy (shared by Context View + Brain Map)

Myli holds a dossier as **12 domains × 6 fixed fact slots**. Six describe the **person**; six describe
their **home journey** (buyer or seller).

### PERSON — who they are
| Domain | Slots |
|--------|-------|
| Family | children · partner · parents · pets · siblings · household |
| Lifestyle | hobbies · fitness · travel · home life · food · entertainment |
| Finance | income · investments · expenses · debt · savings · financial goals |
| Mindset | beliefs · challenges · motivations · fears · growth · attitude |
| Personality | traits · communication · humor · values · quirks · interests |
| Social | network · community · events · clubs/orgs · volunteering · connections |

### HOME — their real-estate journey *(proposed — pending sign-off)*
| Domain | Slots |
|--------|-------|
| Search Criteria | beds · baths · price range · must-haves · style · deal-breakers |
| Current Situation | own/rent · current home · location · equity · listing status · move reason |
| Timeline & Urgency | target date · lease end · urgency · flexibility · season · triggers |
| Budget & Financing | budget · pre-approval · lender · down payment · cash/loan · monthly target |
| Motivation & Goals | why moving · life event · upgrade/downsize · investment · primary goal · dealbreaker |
| Logistics & Process | showings availability · comms pref · decision-makers · agent history · offer readiness · contingencies |

## Context View — PRD

**The button:** "Context" on the AI Agent card. **What it is:** Myli's *read* on the lead — a quick,
human-readable dossier, no message log.

**Contents:**
1. **Sentiment** — current read on the lead's mood/temperature.
2. **DISC profile** — communication style (D/I/S/C) Myli infers, to tailor tone.
3. **Facts** — a scrolling list grouped by the 12 domains above; each fact is a slot + value
   (e.g. Family · Children → "two kids, 8 and 11"). Click a domain to jump to its facts.
4. **Where they stand** — a one-line plain-English status ("Confirmed for two showings, signed the
   rep agreement, ready to go").

**Data:** reads the facts store + sentiment + DISC populated by the extraction engine (SSOT §5).
**Build order:** B3 (after the facts model + extraction engine). **No fabricated facts** — empty
until the engine fills them.

## Brain Map — PRD

**The button:** "Brain Map" on the AI Agent card. **What it is:** the interactive constellation of
everything Myli knows — the visual, explorable version of the dossier.

**Behavior:**
- A **node constellation**: the 12 domains as clusters (PERSON colorway vs HOME colorway), with
  connecting lines. HUD overlay: lead name, stats, recenter, summary, category words.
- **Dark mode / light mode** — the same component, toggleable.
- **Hover** a domain → preview it. **Click** → zoom into that domain's 6 slots. Keep drilling
  **in and out** — domain → slot → fact.
- Reads the same facts store as Context View (domain → slot → value).

**Inspiration:** PlacedAgent's `BrainMapView` (study for UX only — HouseLoop is PlacedAgent-free;
rebuild native, no PA imports). **Build order:** B4 (the big visual lift, after the facts engine).
