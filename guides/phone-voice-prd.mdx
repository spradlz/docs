## Goal

**Own every business number in our own Twilio, capture every call natively (records + notifications), and put
Myli on every line as the front door** — greeting, qualifying intent, filtering spam, booking appointments, and
live-transferring hot leads. Kill the three-source shit-show (FUB / Twilio / Google Voice) and collapse it to one
pipe: **Twilio captures, FUB mirrors, Myli answers.**

## The problem (why this exists)

- Phone-call notifications (Company / Sign / Past-Agent / Ana) are **documented but fire 0** — the trigger
  `phone_call_received` is wired to Twilio, but the numbers route through FUB + Google Voice, so nothing reaches it.
- FUB **call capture broke** at the webhook cutover (stale since ~2026-07-07) and **never captures cold/unknown
  callers** to the company line.
- **Ana's calls run through Google Voice** — invisible to both FUB and Twilio.
- No transcripts exist anywhere (FUB has none).

## The decisions (LOCKED 2026-07-09)

| # | Decision |
|---|---|
| D1 | **Port 3 assets → our Twilio:** Company `913-320-0906`, Sign/Listings `913-354-8600`, Ana `913-954-7123` (out of Google Voice). |
| D2 | **Forward the legacy pool** (`913-453-5505`, `913-270-0645`, +37 past-agent) → ONE dedicated "Legacy Agents" Twilio line. Stay in FUB; do NOT port. |
| D3 | **One notify key `phone_call_received`, 5 subtypes** resolved by dialed-number → `hl_phone_numbers.category` (company/sign/past_agent/ana + generic). Shared resolver used by BOTH the Twilio webhook and the FUB path. |
| D4 | **Ported numbers mirror back into FUB** via `POST /v1/calls` so the FUB timeline stays intact (they leave FUB on port-out). |
| D5 | **Myli is the inbound agent** on all lines — the existing LiveKit + Gemini S2S agent (`apps/lee-voice-agent`), re-pointed. One agent, per-line prompt by DID. |
| D6 | **A2P 10DLC is already registered** under our Twilio account — the ported numbers get **added to the existing campaign**, no new brand/campaign registration needed. |

## Requirements

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="R1 — Ownership & routing">
    - Port D1's three numbers into our Twilio (own them natively).
    - Forward D2's legacy pool → the single "Legacy Agents" Twilio line (same-day FUB config).
    - Caller-ID pass-through on every forward (see who actually called, not the FUB inbox number).
    - Add the ported numbers to the **existing A2P campaign** in Twilio.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="R2 — Capture (Caleb)">
    - Store every call natively on Twilio inbound (metadata + recording + which line), **including cold/unknown
      callers** to the company line.
    - Shared subtype resolver `resolvePhoneCallSubtype(dialedNumber) → hl_phone_numbers.category → subtype`,
      called by both the Twilio webhook and the FUB path.
    - `mirror_to_fub` — write each ported-number call back to FUB (`POST /v1/calls`) with attribution + recording.
    - Google Voice ingest for Ana until her number is ported (then retire GV).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="R3 — Notifications (Lee)">
    - `phone_call_received` fires the correct 1-of-5 typed card by subtype.
    - Lead-less path for cold callers (no person match required).
    - `fub_created` recency gate so a one-off catch-up never notifies on old activity.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="R4 — Myli Voice (Caleb)">
    - One agent, per-line prompt keyed by DID (Company / Sign / Ana / Legacy).
    - Tools: create_lead · book_showing · book_listing_appt · live_transfer · flag_spam · schedule_callback · mirror_to_fub.
    - Intent routing: hot buy/sell → live-transfer + HOT lead; appointment → book; spam → filter; other → callback.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Acceptance criteria

- ☐ Call any of the 4 lines → a lead appears in HouseLoop CRM with the correct source + recording.
- ☐ The matching typed notification (`phone_call_company/sign/past_agent/ana`) fires — no more 0s.
- ☐ A cold unknown caller to the company line is captured + notified (lead-less).
- ☐ A ported-number call also appears on the FUB timeline (mirror).
- ☐ Myli answers with the right per-line intro, qualifies intent, and live-transfers a hot buyer/seller.
- ☐ Ana's calls captured (GV bridge now; native after port).

## Open items / risks

- **FUB `POST /v1/calls`** — confirm the API supports logging a call back to a person (for D4/mirror). Caleb to verify.
- **Google Voice → Twilio port** sometimes rejects direct (VoIP); two-step via a prepaid SIM is the fallback.
- **Legacy line can't distinguish which original number was dialed** — all resolve to `phone_call_past_agent` (FUB still holds the exact number if needed).
