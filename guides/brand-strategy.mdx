## Two Brands, One Business

HouseLoop operates alongside **Spradling Realty Group** (SRG). They serve different purposes
and must never be conflated in advertising or public-facing content.

| | Spradling Realty Group | HouseLoop |
|---|---|---|
| **Role** | Local trust & organic SEO moat | Platform brand for all public-facing content |
| **Google Business Profile** | Yes — 158 reviews, 5 stars, verified | Not yet (future) |
| **Paid ads (Google, FB, IG)** | **NEVER** | Yes — all paid campaigns run under HouseLoop |
| **YouTube** | Legacy channel | HouseLoop (primary going forward) |
| **Instagram** | Legacy | HouseLoop |
| **Facebook** | Legacy | HouseLoop |
| **Google organic / AI search** | Keep — strong ranking for "best realtor in Overland Park" etc. | houseloop.ai pages rank for programmatic SEO (neighborhoods, cities, zip codes) |
| **Landing pages** | None — no new pages on spradlingrealtygroup.com | houseloop.ai is the destination for all campaigns |

## The Local Partner Brand

**"Spradling Group powered by HouseLoop"** — this is the transition branding. The local
team keeps its trust equity (reviews, referrals, relationships), but HouseLoop is the
technology platform behind it. Think: local agent team → powered by HouseLoop's tech stack.

## Why This Matters for Engineering

- **Google Ads account** `369-985-2276` (admin@houseloop.ai) was created for **Keyword Planner API access** (Demand Intelligence product), not for running campaigns under Spradling branding
- When ad campaigns eventually run, landing pages point to **houseloop.ai**, not spradlingrealtygroup.com
- **Google Business Profile** stays as "The Spradling Group - EXP Realty" (or later "Spradling Group powered by HouseLoop") — the 158 reviews and 5-star rating are years of equity
- **SEO article engine** already runs under houseloop.ai — all programmatic SEO pages (city, county, zip, neighborhood, house) are HouseLoop-branded
- **Email templates** in the CRM use HouseLoop branding (`logo-full.png` from `/brand/`)
