## Team-info builders

### Field Overrides
Rename the 'Effective Date' field label (merge code stays $EffectiveDate).

### Reminders & Dates
Reminder Lead Time (days, default 5) applied to all-day Closing & Expire dates; Merge-field date format (e.g. 'Jan 1, 2023 (Sun)' = DATE_LONG_DAY_LAST).

### Payouts
Broker Commissions Payable To + Team Commissions Payable To + Team Split % (% kept from broker, e.g. 80%). (live values: broker = "eXp Realty", team = "The Spradling Group").

### Attachment Folders

- Listing Files
- Contract Files
