The lead detail page (`/leads/[id]`) is where an operator works a single lead. It is the hub for a set of
surfaces that each have their own deep-dive doc — this page explains how they fit together, and links out to
the detail.

<Note>
**Single-scroll redesign (Zach-locked 2026-07-21 → shipped 2026-07-22, Benji).** The page moved from three
independently-scrolling lanes + a self-scrolling "iPad" with a bottom dock, to a **single-scroll, Follow-Up-Boss
style page**: the whole page scrolls as ONE, the center feed is the tall column, the side lanes end and scroll
away, and the iPad frame grows with the conversation. **The composer moved to the TOP of the iPad** (inline, no
modals) and **the old bottom dock became an icon filter row of read-only feed lenses.** Everything below reflects
the new architecture. Source: `components/leads/detail/{CenterLane,LeadComposer,TextComposer,EmailComposer,CallComposer,NoteComposer}.tsx`.

**2026-07-23 batch (Benji):** the FUB-style **contact band** (circle icons that open the composer), the **"From"
line defaulting to the assigned agent**, the **Messages** lens (renamed, now texts + in-app + requests) with
badge-counts that match their lenses, the **AI page** rebuilt (locked AI-Summary format + boxed purple cards),
task cards editable + **times interpreted as Central** (see below). All covered in the sections that follow.
</Note>

## The single-scroll shell

Three columns, but the **whole page scrolls as one** — the center feed defines the page height; the left/right
lanes end and scroll away.

| Lane | What it holds |
|---|---|
| **Left** | The lead + the human agent (and the agent's AI clone). Identity header, the **contact band** (below), agent/collaborators, the **Appointments** + **Tasks** sections (＋ add · card actions), tags, the **AI Mode** toggle. Task cards are the FUB pattern — a complete checkbox (bottom-right), title (**click to edit**), Due **date + year + time**, assignee; times are entered/shown in **Central** (`wallTimeToUtcISO`), never the browser's zone. |
| **Center** (the "iPad") | The **inline composer** at the top, then the **icon filter row**, then the **feed** — grows with the conversation. |
| **Right** | **Myli**, the assigned AI agent — status, model, cost, and the big **Brain Map** button. |

The left and right lanes share the same primitives so they stack pixel-identically — see
[Lead Detail — Parity Lanes](/guides/lead-detail-parity-lanes) and
[Lead Detail — AI Agent](/guides/lead-detail-ai-agent).

## The contact band (FUB-style circles, Zach 2026-07-22)

The left-lane contact rows use **colored circle icon buttons** on the left of each line — matching the sacred green
call button — instead of uppercase labels (`components/leads/detail/ContactCircle.tsx`):

- 🟢 **Green circle = Call** · 🔵 **Blue circle = Text / iMessage / Email** · 🔵 **Blue circle = Address** (a new
  `hl_leads.address` field, mig 0447). Clicking a circle **opens the matching section of the top composer** (call →
  Make Call, email → Send Email) — **no modal**. The email **wraps in full** so a long address is selectable/copyable.
- Below the contact lines, **one divider**, then a second group: **Integration · Lead Source · Relationship** (they
  keep their labels).
- **Myli's lane mirrors it** — the same green/blue circles on her Call/iMessage/Email lines, but **inert** (she's not
  someone you dial), so the two lanes line up. `ContactCircle` renders a `<button>` on the agent lane, a `<span>` on Myli.

Locked wireframe: `apps/houseloop-crm/docs/wireframes/lead-contact-band-LOCKED-2026-07-22.html`.

## The inline composer (top of the iPad)

Composing is **always available at the top** — no overlay modals. A tab strip switches between:

**✦ AI Summary · Send Text · Send Email · Make Call · Create Note.** The composer **defaults to AI Summary on
every load** (and resets to it when the lead changes) — read the lead before you write. The contact-band circles
(below) open the matching composer tab, so nothing pops a modal.

- **AI Summary** reads Myli's real dossier (`hl_lead_dossier` via `GET /api/leads/[id]/context`) — the SAME source
  the AI lens renders (one summary, two places). **↻ Refresh** rebuilds it (`POST /context` → one LLM extraction
  over the lead's HouseLoop history + **FUB facts** via `loadFubBrief`). The summary follows a **locked format**
  (Zach 2026-07-23): a 2-sentence read → **Key Insights** (🏡 goal · 💰 personality/decision style · 📍 current
  stage · ⚡ risk/opportunity) → **Next Best Action** (one sentence — the immediate follow-up only; never
  operations/financing). Rendered by the shared `AiSummaryText` component.
- **Send Text** — To (name → number) · **From = the A2P-compliant company lines** (`/api/phone-numbers`, `a2p===true`)
  + the iMessage line · ✦ AI-generate tabs (Introduction / Follow Up / Still Buying / Nurture Lead / **+ Custom**
  voice-mic modal) · **Attach vCard / Biz-Card** (renders a chip in the body, sends with the message).
- **Send Email** — inline To/From/Subject, AI-generate tabs, live signature preview, Send Email.
- **Make Call** — inline, dual-mode: **Make Call** (rings your mobile then bridges the lead → an **on-call screen
  with a live timer + notes**; on hangup — detected via `/api/calls/status` polling — the notes become a
  **call-note** in the feed) and **Log Call** (record a past call). Green **Call** button (the Phone icon).
- **Create Note** — inline textarea + a blue **Create Note** button.

**The `+ Custom` modal** is the one pink/purple (`--hl-ai`) overlay: a big prompt + a Web-Speech **mic** → `/ai-draft`.

### The "From" default — the assigned agent's own line (Zach 2026-07-22)

Because a **human** is composing, the Text and Call "From" default to the **assigned agent's own line**, not the
AI-send resolver (which blanks when the AI mic is off). Order (`GET /api/leads/[id]/send-identity`, `defaultSenderId`
for Text + `defaultVoiceNumber` for Call): (1) the assigned agent's text/voice-default dialer (or their default
BlueBubbles line for iMessage) — reassign the agent, reopen, it follows; (2) no assigned agent (company/inbound
lead) → **the number the lead came in on** (last inbound `hl_calls.to_number`, e.g. the company line). Myli's own
auto-sends are unaffected (they still resolve through `resolveAgentSendIdentity`).

## The icon filter row (read-only feed lenses)

Below the composer, an **icon row** (icon over a single-word label + a count badge) filters the ONE feed. It
replaced the old bottom dock. Order:

**All · ✦ AI · Messages · Emails · Calls · Notes · Appts · Activity · Starred · Log · ⌃ FUB (far right).**

- **The middle column is READ-ONLY** — no create/send buttons in any lens. Creating/sending comes from the top
  composer and the side lanes (the ＋ on the left-lane Appointments section).
- Count badges are always visible and **turn red when there's something NEW**. **Every count matches what its lens
  renders** — the badge and the display derive from the SAME kind-list (`lib/leads/feed-lenses.ts`), so a badge can
  never disagree with its lens (this fixed "Text said 1 with 0 shown / Activity said 8 with 2 shown", Zach 2026-07-23).

<Note>
**Messages vs Activity — "said" vs "did" (Zach 2026-07-23).** **Messages** (renamed from "Text") is **everything the
lead sent that needs a reply** — SMS + iMessage + **Zillow in-app messages** + **requests** (tour/contact/property/
seller inquiries) — rendered as ONE time-ordered feed (reply from the composer at the top; `AllStreamV2 kinds=MESSAGE_KINDS`).
**Activity** is the **record of website behaviour** — views/likes/saves/searches/registration — **plus the same
requests + in-app** (a request is also a thing they did on the site). So requests + in-app are **dual-listed** (Messages
because they need a reply, Activity because they're a website record), and everything appears in **All**. The Zillow
in-app card's action is **"View in Follow Up Boss"** (Zillow has no per-lead page) — the broken "Look up on HouseLoop" was removed.
</Note>

- **✦ AI** = the AI page (`LeadContextPanel` `initialView="context"`) — see below. **FUB** (far right, the 3-chevron
  logo) = the imported Follow Up Boss thread (`initialView="fub"`); the old "Lead Context / FUB Activity" pills inside
  the AI lens were removed.
- **Starred** = rows the user pinned with the per-row ☆ (`hl_lead_activity_star`, per user). **Log** = the
  change/audit kinds (stage/name/contact/tag/deal). **Notes** = human notes only (excludes `fact_captured`
  AI-memory). **Emails** reads the real email log so older sends still show.
- **Appts** = the appointments view (all appointments, no create button — the ＋ is on the left lane). Left-lane
  appointment cards have a **View · Edit · Disposition** action row: View → the middle Appts column, Edit → the
  edit modal (this appointment loaded), Disposition → the outcome modal.

<Note>
Modals (appointment create/edit, etc.) center over the **visible viewport** of the iPad — the anchor rect is
clamped to the screen, because the single-scroll iPad is a tall column (unclamped, a modal's Save button fell
off the bottom).
</Note>

## The AI page (the ✦ AI lens)

The **AI** lens (`LeadContextPanel`, `components/leads/detail/LeadContextPanel.tsx`) is a single cohesive
**Myli-purple** (`--hl-ai` #A855F7) surface — reworked 2026-07-23. Top to bottom:

1. **AI Summary** — the dossier summary, at the very top. One-line header: a Sparkles AI icon + bold purple
   **AI Summary**, then a smaller grey "Myli's AI summary about `<lead>` · updated `<date/time>`" beside it, and
   **↻ Refresh** on the right. The body renders the locked format (2-sentence read → **Key Insights** emoji bullets
   → **Next Best Action**) via the shared `AiSummaryText` component — the SAME summary the composer's AI Summary tab shows.
2. **Facts Myli Tracks** — one big purple AI section header, then a **3-column grid of boxed cards**, all the same
   treatment: the read cards **Sentiment · DISC Profile · Where they stand**, then each fact **domain** with facts
   (**Search Criteria · Current Situation · Timeline & Urgency · Budget & Financing · Motivation & Goals · Logistics
   & Process**, plus person domains). Each card = a purple AI-icon header + its `slot: value` facts; **only learned
   facts show** (no "not learned yet"). Shared `AiCard` primitive. The 6 home domains come straight from the fact
   taxonomy (`lib/ai/fact-taxonomy.ts`) — no prompt change needed to produce them.
3. **Myli Memory Updates** — the working-memory stream (`MemoryStream`), header + each entry title ("Updated working
   memory") in the same purple AI-icon treatment.

The summary + facts are generated by `buildLeadContext` (`lib/ai/extract-context.ts`) over the lead's HouseLoop
history **and** FUB facts, written to `hl_lead_dossier` + `hl_lead_facts`. See [Lead Detail — AI Agent](/guides/lead-detail-ai-agent).

## Modes render a band above the lanes

Two modes drop a full-width **dashboard band above the three lanes** — both the same fixed height, different
content — while the iPad below stays identical. Modes are driven by the URL `view` param (no new routes):

| `?view=` | Band shown | Toggle |
|---|---|---|
| `agent` (default) | none — just the iPad | — |
| `tc` | **TC Dashboard band** | the left-lane **Agent ⟷ TC** switch |
| `referral` | **Referral Dashboard band** | the left-lane **Active ⟷ Referral** switch |

The Referral view is a *view*, not a status flag — it can be opened on an active client as well as a past client.
Both bands share one fixed height, so switching modes never shifts the lanes below.

### TC Dashboard band

Shown in `?view=tc` (the **Agent ⟷ TC** toggle). It holds the whole transaction; the iPad below stays the active
baseline. One fixed-height band = a navy header (hero + Buyer/Seller switch + 6 fact cards) → the dated milestone
stepper → the TC docket.

**The band has TWO chapters, driven by the loop's state** — the same band, lit differently:

**Chapter 1 — Shopping** (the Agency Loop, before a contract). The transaction chrome is *present but dimmed* —
the Coordinator/Status dropdowns, List/Contract prices, and dates read `n/a until UC` because they don't apply
yet. The stepper is the short agency spine: **Agency Prep → Agreement Sent → Agreement Signed → Under Contract
("loads loop")**. This is where a buyer lives while they're still shopping.

![TC Dashboard band — Chapter 1, Shopping (dimmed, agency stepper)](/images/lead-detail-tc-shopping.png)

**Chapter 2 — Under Contract** (the moment the `Under Contract` milestone is *completed* — a merely-scheduled UC
date does not flip it). The chrome **lights up**: the Buyer segment shows a green **UNDER CONTRACT** dot, the
Coordinator + a live **Status** pill appear, the price/date cards go active, and the stepper becomes the full
14-step transaction tracker: **Under Contract → Earnest Money Due → Inspection Ends → Inspection Resolution →
Title → Appraisal → Clear to Close → Final Walkthrough → Closing → Funded → Possession.** The loop tab now shows
the property address instead of "Agency Loop."

![TC Dashboard band — Chapter 2, Under Contract (lit, full 14-step stepper)](/images/lead-detail-tc-undercontract.png)

The band always shows a populated chapter for a lead that has a loop — it defaults to the agency (shopping) loop
and flips to under-contract as the milestone completes; `?loop=<id>` selects a specific loop.

| Region | What it is |
|---|---|
| **Hero** (top-left) | "TC Dashboard" + `Client · <name>` |
| **Buyer/Seller switch** | Two segments, each showing that side's status — a **green dot = Under Contract** on that side, grey = no live file, muted = no loop that side. The selected side (white) drives the stepper (via `?loop=<id>`). |
| **6 fact cards** | `[Coordinator ▾ / Status ▾]` (stacked dropdowns) · **Side** · **List Price** · **Contract Price** · **Under Contract Date** · **Closing Date**. Cards 2–6 are label-on-top / big-number-below. Prices format `$785,000` under \$1M, `$1.25M` at/above. |
| **Milestone stepper** | The collapsed 14-step dated tracker: Agency Prep · Agr Sent · Agr Signed · **UC** · EMD Due · Inspect Ends · Inspect Res · Title · Appraisal · Clear · Walkthru · Closing · Funded · Possession. Date under each node; done = blue, current = ring, pending = grey. Click an open node to complete it (forward-only, advances the stage). |
| **TC docket** (line under the stepper) | A horizontal action bar — a blue **Transaction Summary** scroll-through hub + 📋 Tasks · 📄 Documents · ✉️ Emails · 📎 Attachments · 📅 Dates · 👥 Participants · 📝 Notes/Activities · 💰 Payout · 🗂️ Misc. Each opens a full modal (the iPad never changes). Catalog-driven — a new surface is one registry entry. |

A deal is **added on the iPad Deals tab**, not here — the band renders the transaction, it doesn't create one.
Header + stepper data from `GET /api/tc/loops/:id`. **Transaction Summary** pages the A-Frame collection panels
(Client Info · Contract · Inspections · Financing · Commission · Listing Summary) — the same surfaces as Adam's
global `/tc/*` transaction view (one data model, two front doors). Component: `components/leads/detail/MilestoneBand.tsx`.

### Referral Dashboard band

Shown in `?view=referral` (the **Active ⟷ Referral** toggle). It holds the relationship + the referral flywheel.

![Referral Dashboard band — the toggle turned on above the iPad](/images/lead-detail-referral-band.png)

| Region | What it is |
|---|---|
| **Hero** | "Referral Dashboard" + `Client · <name>` |
| **Relationship** (top-right) | A dropdown for the client's relationship, sourced live from the customizable **[Relationships builder](/guides/relationships)** (Settings → Pipeline & Workflow → Relationships) — scoped to the viewer. Every agent sees the base set **Lead · Sphere of Influence · Past Client**; admins also see admin-scoped customs (e.g. Ana Past Client). Origin (where the lead came from) stays separate on the lead source — the old conflated `relationship_bucket` enum was replaced 2026-07-10. |
| **6 KPIs** | Touches · Last Touch · Referrals · Lifetime Closings · Lifetime Volume · Lifetime Value. The lifetime metrics attribute every **closed** deal across all referrals this client has given (`hl_lead_referral_rollup`). |
| **Add or View your referrals** | Opens the referrals table (view + add) — the same table as the Referrals KPI. |
| **Referral Priority** | A/B/C/D pills (A = best). |
| **Dossier** | A large free-text field — the story of the relationship. |

Bucket, priority, and dossier persist via `PATCH /api/leads/:id`. Component: `components/leads/detail/ReferralBand.tsx`.

## AI Mode (per lead, per surface)

Each lead has an **AI Mode** toggle in the left lane. When on, inbound texts/emails for the lead are dispatched
to the assigned AI agent (Myli by default) — the human owner is still notified; AI Mode adds the AI loop, it does
not suppress the human. The AI cockpit (Active Triggers → Current Workflow → Current Action) on the right lane is
gated on this flag. See [Lead Detail — AI Agent](/guides/lead-detail-ai-agent).

## Deep-linking

Every surface is addressable via URL params so a notification or another screen can open the lead exactly where
it should land:

```
/leads/[id]?view=<agent|tc|referral>&loop=<loopId>&tab=<Tab>&subtab=<SubTab>&focus=<id>
```

Full param reference: [CRM Deep-Linking](/guides/lead-detail-routing).
