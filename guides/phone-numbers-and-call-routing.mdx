## The North Star

**All comms run through HouseLoop.** FUB is no longer where we talk to people — every call, text,
and email flows through HouseLoop / **Myli**. FUB's only remaining roles: (1) a **data source**
(past-client inbound on the kept Past-Agent numbers, captured to our DB), and (2) a parking lot for a
couple of unused legacy dialer numbers. Same de-risk-from-FUB logic as the walled-garden doctrine —
FUB is Zillow-owned and progressively closing its data taps, so we own the numbers and own the conversation.

## Inbound capture — every call & text lands on the lead

<Check>
**Live (shipped 2026-06-22).** Inbound calls, texts, and emails are stored to the lead record and
threaded onto the conversation — **including callers who aren't a lead yet.**
</Check>

The root cause we fixed: FUB delivers unknown callers with **`personId = 0`**, and the webhook handler did
`if (!personId) return` — and `!0` is `true`, so **every missed call, voicemail, and first-touch text from
an unknown number was silently dropped.** Now `handleCall` / `handleTextMessage` / `handleEmail`
(`apps/dashboard/server/fub-webhooks.ts`) store `person_id = 0` as an *unmatched* record keyed by phone
number instead of dropping it. So the full inbox — missed calls and voicemails included — is captured and
converts cleanly to a lead when the caller is identified.

| Inbound type | Captured | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Call** (incl. missed / voicemail) | ✅ | Recording URL + outcome stored; transcript when available |
| **Text** (SMS / iMessage) | ✅ | Threaded onto the lead; unknown-number texts captured too |
| **Email** | ✅ | Threaded onto the lead |
