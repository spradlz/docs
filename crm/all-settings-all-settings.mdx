**All Settings** (`/settings`) is the configuration hub for the HouseLoop CRM. It mirrors the sidebar's
settings groups. Every area below is a live settings page; click through to its documentation.

## User Settings
| Setting | What it does |
|---|---|
| [Profile Settings](/crm/user-settings-profile-settings) | Your name, email, avatar, password. |
| [Business Settings](/crm/user-settings-business-settings) | Logo, brand colors, company identity, business hours. |
| [Notifications](/guides/notifications) | Email + SMS notification preferences, per event. |

## Team Settings
| Setting | What it does |
|---|---|
| [My Team](/crm/team-settings-my-team) | Team members, roles, invites. |
| [Audit Log](/crm/team-settings-audit-log) | Who changed what, when. |
