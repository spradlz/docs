### Problem
The lead-detail **Texts** dock used three flat pills — `Lead · Msale | Agent · Myli | Both` — which tangle two
different questions: *who the conversation is with* and *which slice of it you're watching*. Zach: "these buttons
don't make sense." And `Both` rendered one interleaved column, not a real dual lane.

### The locked model — TWO tiers

### Tier 1 — who you're talking to (top split)
