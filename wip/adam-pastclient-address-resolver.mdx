##### THE PROBLEM
Past-client homes come from **typed source data** (old CRM/SISU entries), so the street is frequently garbled — a
wrong house number, a misspelled or abbreviated street, or a **missing city**. A missing city is the worst: CASS
then geocodes the fragment to the **wrong state entirely** (`105 E Washington` with no city → Attica, **Indiana**).
Address text alone cannot be trusted, so the address-only reconciliation engine (identityKey → LLM → Redfin) can
land on the wrong rooftop or mint a fork.

##### THE CORE INSIGHT — the MLS# is the ground-truth anchor
You **cannot search** Zillow by MLS number, but Zillow's **property-details** page carries it
(`attributionInfo.mlsId`). So the resolver treats the MLS# as truth and the typed address as a hint:
resolve an address → get the `zpid` → read `property-details(zpid).attributionInfo.mlsId` → **validate it against
our known MLS#(s)** for the home = 100% certainty it's the right rooftop, not "probably."

###### THE MISKEY LESSON (LOCKED 2026-07-02) — the MLS# overrides the typed address, every time
When the typed source address and the MLS#-derived Zillow address disagree, **the MLS# wins.** Real fixes:
| Typed source (wrong) | MLS#-anchored truth |
|---|---|
| 13673 Hallett (no city) | **12673 S Hallet St, Olathe KS 66062** (MLS 2040181) |
| 72245 Widmer St | **7224 Widmer Rd, Shawnee KS 66216** (extra digit + St→Rd) |
| Blue Stem | **1226 Bluestem Dr, Eudora KS 66025** (one word) |
| 105 E Washington (no city → Attica, IN) | **105 E Washington St, Paola KS 66071** |

##### THE RESOLVER (per home; ran on all ~1,936)
1. **CASS/USPS clean** the typed address — Google Address Validation, HouseLoop key `GOOGLE_GEO_SERVER_KEY`
   (project houseloop-496200). Fixes wrong ZIP + garble. 99.9% validate.
2. **Find the Zillow record** — OpenWeb Ninja multi-form search: CASS `street, zip` → `street, city, STATE zip`
   (CASS mangles rural/garbled streets) → street-type variants (St/Ter/Dr/Ct/Ln) → range-preserved
   (`10700-10702`) → original. Pick the candidate by **house# + street-name-token overlap**. Multi-unit buildings:
   pick the unit from Zillow's autocomplete **dropdown** → the unit's own `zpid` (a location string with `APT n`
   won't match).
3. **Validate via MLS#** — `property-details(zpid).attributionInfo.mlsId` vs our known MLS#s
   (`hl_srg_past_client_homes.mls_number` ∪ `hl_mls_property_history` ∪ `website_hlid \d+`).
4. **Classify (never silently wrong):** `confirmed` (MLS-locked) · `address_match` (house#+street verified, Zillow
   exposed no mlsId — normal for older sales) · `mls_mismatch` (flag) · `not_found` (flag).

##### THE VALUE — five buckets, all refreshed monthly, differing only in the monthly ENGINE
| Bucket | When | Value | Monthly engine |
|---|---|---|---|
| `ninja_monthly` | sold, has a cached Zestimate | zestimate × 1.005 | re-fetch Zestimate (OpenWeb Ninja) |
| `zhvi_monthly` | sold, no Zestimate | sold price × ZHVI area appreciation | ZHVI recompute |
| `active` | currently for-sale/pending/coming-soon | list × 1.005 | list re-read; flips to `ninja_monthly` on close |
| `native` | HL-organic card from our sold data, not on Zillow (12111 W 165th Ter) | last value × ZHVI appreciation | ZHVI recompute |
| `self_sourced_card` | new-construction not on Zillow (1809 SW Hightown) | our MLS sold × ZHVI appreciation | ZHVI recompute |
| `self_sourced_card_land` | raw land (00000 Old Kansas City Rd) | sale price (ZHVI is a home index) | held at sale price |

"Native" = *native value source*, not *no monthly update*. Out-of-area homes (closed with us but outside our
display MLS) get a card + ZHVI value but **no live IDX listing** (no display authority).
