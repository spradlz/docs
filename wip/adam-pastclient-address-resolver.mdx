##### THE PROBLEM
Past-client homes come from **typed source data** (old CRM/SISU entries), so the street is frequently garbled тАФ a
wrong house number, a misspelled or abbreviated street, or a **missing city**. A missing city is the worst: CASS
then geocodes the fragment to the **wrong state entirely** (`105 E Washington` with no city тЖТ Attica, **Indiana**).
Address text alone cannot be trusted, so the address-only reconciliation engine (identityKey тЖТ LLM тЖТ Redfin) can
land on the wrong rooftop or mint a fork.

##### THE CORE INSIGHT тАФ the MLS# is the ground-truth anchor
You **cannot search** Zillow by MLS number, but Zillow's **property-details** page carries it
(`attributionInfo.mlsId`). So the resolver treats the MLS# as truth and the typed address as a hint:
resolve an address тЖТ get the `zpid` тЖТ read `property-details(zpid).attributionInfo.mlsId` тЖТ **validate it against
our known MLS#(s)** for the home = 100% certainty it's the right rooftop, not "probably."

###### THE MISKEY LESSON (LOCKED 2026-07-02) тАФ the MLS# overrides the typed address, every time
When the typed source address and the MLS#-derived Zillow address disagree, **the MLS# wins.** Real fixes:

|---|---|
| 13673 Hallett (no city) | **12673 S Hallet St, Olathe KS 66062** (MLS 2040181) |
| 72245 Widmer St | **7224 Widmer Rd, Shawnee KS 66216** (extra digit + StтЖТRd) |
| Blue Stem | **1226 Bluestem Dr, Eudora KS 66025** (one word) |
