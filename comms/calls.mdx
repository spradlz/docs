**Calls** is the voice channel: a lead (or anyone) dials a HouseLoop number, the call is answered — by a
teammate, by a forward, or by the AI voice agent — and what happened is captured on the lead's timeline.

## How a call flows

1. A call lands on a HouseLoop number (see **[Phone Numbers & Call Routing](/guides/phone-numbers-and-call-routing)**).
2. Routing decides who answers: a forward to a teammate's cell, the dialer default, or the **AI voice agent**.
3. If enabled, the call is **recorded** and (with AI analysis on) transcribed and summarized.
4. The call — direction, duration, recording, disposition, and any AI summary — is written to the lead's
   activity, and an inbound call fires a notification.

For the end-to-end architecture (the voice webhook order, Twilio signature verification, idempotency), see the
comms subsystem map: **[CRM Comms — Texts, Calls, iMessage](/guides/crm-comms)**.
