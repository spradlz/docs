**Text** is the messaging channel: a lead texts a HouseLoop number, or a teammate / the AI agent texts them
back, and the whole back-and-forth threads on the lead. HouseLoop sends over **two rails** — **iMessage**
(blue, via a self-hosted BlueBubbles server) and **SMS** (green, via Twilio) — and picks the rail from the
sender's default channel, falling back to SMS when iMessage is unavailable.

## What's in here

## How a text flows

1. A send resolves an **identity** (a teammate or Myli) and that identity's **default text channel**.
2. iMessage available → it goes out **blue** over BlueBubbles; otherwise it falls back to **green** SMS over Twilio.
3. Inbound and outbound both land in the one messaging table (`hl_messages`, channel `sms` or `imessage`) and
   render in the lead's **Texts** thread.
4. An inbound text fires a notification.

The numbers the texts ride on — and how a number is assigned or ported — live in **[Phone Numbers, Call Routing
& Number Porting](/guides/phone-numbers-and-call-routing)**. For the end-to-end architecture (the one-writer
`logMessage` law, inbound routing by destination identity, send-identity resolution), see the comms subsystem
map: **[CRM Comms — Texts, Calls, iMessage](/guides/crm-comms)**.
