<Info>
**The native integration.** houseloop.ai is our own site, so this pipe needs no API key — visitors become leads
through [Website Registration & Lead Intake](/guides/web-registration-and-intake), each action fires one of the
**9 registration doors** below, and every door's messaging follows **the two-beat law**. Door on/off + workflow
switches live on [Trigger Events](/guides/trigger-events). Live-fire verified 2026-07-09 (all 9 doors, both
phone variants, timing measured).
</Info>

## The two-beat law

Every new registrant gets exactly two beats:

1. **Beat 1 — the branded acknowledgment, instantly.** Email from **HouseLoop &lt;updates@houseloop.ai&gt;** +
   an ack text from the **branded line (913) 286-1761**. Corporate voice, signed "— HouseLoop." Never a person.
2. **Beat 2 — Myli, personally, at ~2 minutes.** Email from **Myli &lt;myli@houseloop.ai&gt;** + a text from
   **her own line** (BlueBubbles iMessage by default; her Member Connections toggle can flip her to Twilio SMS —
   the toggle drives the live send path). Human voice, referencing exactly what the person did or asked — the
   `ai.*` sections are **composed live per lead** by Myli, each with an editable static fallback. She never says
   "an agent will reach out" (the no-handoff law — she books directly).

Sender identities are configuration, not code: the brand line = `notification_sender.sms_from_number`, Myli's
line = her dialer/BlueBubbles assignment. **No phone number is hardcoded anywhere** (verified: zero grep hits).

## The canvas shape (every door, identical skeleton)

```
stage → (door seed/note) → BRAND EMAIL (Send as: HouseLoop) → phone branch
  ├─ has phone:  BRAND ACK SMS → wait 2m → MYLI EMAIL → MYLI SMS → done
  └─ no phone:   wait 2m → MYLI EMAIL → done   (texts correctly skipped, never a failed run)
```

Every send step declares its sender explicitly on the canvas; the phone branch means a no-phone registrant
still gets both emails (verified: run completes, 2-minute gap measured 121–146s across all doors).
