## The no-response cascade (proven 2026-07-06, gated off)

Myli is never idle ‚Äî after every touch she leaves a **floor**: one scheduled future reach-out. How fast that floor comes back is driven by the lead's **stage**, and the aggressive "chase a new lead" behavior is folded into the same one scheduler ‚Äî it is NOT a separate engine:

- A brand-new lead (**New Lead** / **Attempted Contact**) gets a **daily** touch.
- After **4 un-answered** touches in a row, Myli **auto-moves them to Nurture Warm**, which reprices the floor to **every 2 weeks**. Keep going quiet ‚Üí she keeps easing off (Warm ‚Üí Cold ‚Üí monthly).
- The moment the lead **replies**, the streak resets to zero and she re-engages.

Everything is visible and editable: the cadence + the "after N attempts, move to X stage" rule live in one config row (`hl_settings.ai_closed_loop.cadence`), and every auto-move writes an audited stage-change row you can see on the lead. Proven end-to-end on a test lead (under-cap stays daily ¬∑ at-cap moves to Warm + reprices ¬∑ a reply resets the streak). Shipped dormant ‚Äî nothing fires on real leads until the master flag is flipped.

## The two-beat registration model (LAW 1 ‚Äî shipped on all 11 registration doors, proven 2026-07-06)

Every registration or inquiry now sends **two beats**, in order:

- **Beat 1 ‚Äî the brand ack.** An instant, agent-agnostic "HouseLoop Team, we got your request" email. No personal name, no personal number, no signature ‚Äî it's from the brand. This is the receipt.
- **Beat 2 ‚Äî the personal beat.** Myli's own message (or your clone's, if configured) that actually moves things forward and books directly. This is the only beat that's allowed to be personal.

It's built the auditable way: Beat 1 is a **visible first step on each intent canvas** (brand ack ‚Üí the New/Existing branch ‚Üí Myli's personal beat), reusing the brand templates that already exist ‚Äî nothing hidden, nothing hardcoded. **Live-proven**: a real tour request fired the brand `tour_confirmation` first, then Myli's personal `beta_tour_request` email, and the run completed clean.

Shipped on **all 11 registration/inquiry doors** ‚Äî tour, contact, liked, saved, saved-search, shared, general registration, seller get-started, home-value report, property inquiry, and rental inquiry. Two batches, both live-proven (tour request fired the brand ack + Myli's personal beat; general registration fired its brand ack, run completed). Pure behavior events (viewed a home, virtually toured) don't get a brand ack ‚Äî there's no "request" to acknowledge.

## The follow-up engine ‚Äî the full end-to-end build (2026-07-06)

Everything below shipped in one pass. All of it is **gated OFF for real leads** (config flags / test-lead scope) and proven on the controlled test lead. It's the machinery that makes Myli own the relationship from first touch to booked showing and beyond.

**1. The never-idle floor + the no-response cascade.** After any touch, Myli leaves a scheduled next touch ("the floor"), paced by the lead's stage. A brand-new lead who goes quiet gets a daily nudge; after 4 unanswered touches she auto-moves them to Nurture Warm, which slows her to every 2 weeks ‚Äî the aggressive chase and the gentle nurture are *one* stage-driven dial you can see and edit, and every auto-move is an audited line on the lead. Proven: under-cap stays daily, at-cap moves + reprices, a reply resets the counter.

**2. The daily hand-curated home.** For an actively-shopping lead (with no pending showing), Myli sends one hand-picked active home per day, tied to their saved search, deduped so she never repeats one, framed as her own find (never "I saw you browsing"). It respects the pause ‚Äî nothing goes out while a showing is booked. Proven across all its gates.
