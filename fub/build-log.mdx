## What we're building

Every FUB signal (tour, contact, saved search, view/save/share, Zillow intent tags, insight notes) becomes a
first-class `hl_lead_activity` event. **Requests create the lead; behavior events backfill it completely on
pull-in + stay updated forward.** Behavior-Based Follow-Up, not "hot." We document Zillow's playbooks; we don't
replicate them.
