## One card, three kinds, every surface

There is **one** `RequestCard` component, kind-driven, rendered identically on **FUB Activity · Website
Activity · All Activity** (and mirrored in the Telegram alert formatting). No accept/decline buttons — the card
is display-only; acting on a request is a [workflow](/guides/workflows), not a button.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Property Inquiry" icon="house">
    Photo · full address · MLS · price/beds · message. No calendar chip.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tour Request" icon="calendar-check">
    Same + a calendar chip (showing date/time) + "View all tour requests".
  </Card>
  <Card title="Saved Search" icon="bookmark">
    Criteria line — property types · beds · baths · price · for-rent · location.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

The data is all in `fub.events.property` (street, city, zip, MLS, price, beds, lat/lng, Zillow URL) — see the
[Data & Signals Matrix](/fub/data-matrix). Photos resolve from our `Listing` DB, falling back to the Zillow
`zpid` for out-of-market homes.
