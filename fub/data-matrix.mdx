## The principle (LOCKED)

**Every FUB signal becomes one of OUR OWN events** — minted via `logActivity()` into the one run-log
`hl_lead_activity` (no new store, no fork). `fub.*` is the raw landing zone; our event store is the truth.
Once a signal is our event it's our data, it renders on our [cards](/fub/request-cards), and it can trigger
our workflows.

## What this page will cover

<Steps>
  <Step title="The signal inventory">Every distinct FUB signal with live volume — Property Inquiry, Viewed/Saved Property, Seller Inquiry, saved searches, insight notes, ML intent tags — and the HouseLoop event kind each maps to.</Step>
  <Step title="The coarse-type problem">FUB stores all property contact as one `type='Property Inquiry'`; the real intent (tour / inquiry / contact / rental / seller) lives only in the message + source. One `classifyFubEvent()` resolves it everywhere.</Step>
  <Step title="The Zillow high-intent tag playbook">High Intent Buyer (permanent) / High Intent Seller (removed after 13 days) / Smart List (rolling 13-day) → `hot_buyer` / `hot_seller` / `smart_list_hot` events; tag removal is itself a "cooled off" signal.</Step>
  <Step title="The insight notes">"may be interested … viewed N× in 7 days" + "now looking in &lt;area&gt;" arrive as notes, currently raw → parse into structured intent events.</Step>
</Steps>
