## 1 · Lead Integrations

**An integration is the PIPE a signal arrives through** (HouseLoop.ai · Follow Up Boss). Its settings decide only
whether HouseLoop acts on an event and whether it mints a lead — **never** notifications (those are source-agnostic,
§4).

### 1a · Follow Up Boss (via the FUB mirror) — LOCKED 2026-07-07

The FUB scraper mirrors a lead's Zillow/FUB activity into `fub.*` — a **Zillow pixel for FUB leads**. The importer
(`fub-lead-import.ts`) reads the mirror and routes each event. The FUB Settings page (`/settings/fub`) has **two**
per-event toggles:

| Toggle | Means | DB |
|---|---|---|
| **Sync** *(was "Process")* | Does HouseLoop act on this FUB event at all (off = ignore; raw data still mirrors). | `enabled` |
| **Creates Lead** | When syncing, if the person isn't already a lead, mint one (off = only append to existing). | `creates_lead` |

- **Minting rule:** a brand-new person becomes a lead **only** when Sync ON **and** Creates Lead ON. Existing leads
  never dupe (matched on `fub_person_id`) and their full history backfills regardless of per-event toggles
  (step 2.6, catalog-driven from `fub.events`).
- **The NOTIFY + NOTIFY-VIA columns are GONE** — notifications are source-agnostic (§4), governed only by
  `/settings/notifications`. Removed end-to-end (UI, API, importer, dashboard `fubGate`); the orphaned
  `notify` / `notify_owner` DB columns await a typed-approved drop.
- **RealLoop notifier RETIRED** — the dashboard's `notifyZach*` (Telegram + Twilio SMS) double-fired and its
  catch-up pollers bypassed the gate. Master-killed at source (`REALLOOP_NOTIFY_ENABLED`, default off). HouseLoop is
  the **sole** notifier.
- **MVP flow:** Sync + Creates Lead ON for **Tour Request only** → a tour pulls the person in, backfills their whole
  FUB history, and every later view/inquiry lands on their timeline labeled *"from Zillow via Follow Up Boss."*

### 1b · Platform / Source model

Every lead carries `platform` (the integration — `follow_up_boss` / `houseloop`) and `source` (the brand — `Zillow`
/ `houseloop.ai`). Both are lead attributes, both agnostic — printed into every notification (§4d).

## 2 · Trigger Events (the doors)

**A trigger event is a DOOR — unique per integration.** "FUB · Property Inquiry" ≠ "HL · Property Inquiry." Every
door belongs to ONE integration and answers two questions: does it pull the person into HouseLoop, and which
workflow runs. The vocabulary that unlocked this (LOCKED 2026-07-07):

| Word | Means | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Integration** | The **pipe** a signal arrives through | HouseLoop.ai · Follow Up Boss |
| **Lead Source** | The **brand** on the lead | Zillow · HouseLoop Organic · Referral |
| **Trigger Event** | A **door** — unique per integration | FUB · Property Inquiry |
| **Workflow** | A **playbook** — one per door, 1:1 | HL · Liked Home Registration |

**The Trigger Events screen** groups doors by integration: HouseLoop.ai gets a registration/behavior split
(21 doors); Follow Up Boss is ONE flat list (23 doors — everyone's already in FUB); TC gets 11.

## 3 · Workflows — The Lead Engine (LOCKED 2026-07-07)

**One door = one playbook, make.com style.** A workflow is a short, straight top-to-bottom line (2–4 steps) — no
routers, no shared canvases. ~50 doors, each 1:1 with its playbook.

### 3a · The three laws

1. **Every door runs its own playbook** — one integration, one workflow, a straight line. No routers.
2. **"Creates a Lead" is its own toggle** — separate from the playbook. Only matters when the person doesn't already
   exist; if they do, the door matches them (no duplicate, ever) and the playbook still runs.
3. **Myli always watches** — outside every playbook, anyone texting Myli's line gets Myli; agents' lines get their
   clones. Killed only by the one master switch.

## 4 · Notifications (the delivery layer)

Notifications ride **under** the spine — a door fires, the event routes to its notification class, and delivery is
governed in **exactly one place** (`/settings/notifications`), source- and integration-agnostic, owner-only. Deep
catalog: [Lee Messages & Notifications Catalog](/platform/message-catalog).

### 4a · The One-Card System — ONE shell, payload-driven (LOCKED 2026-07-06)

Agent notifications are ONE shell + a payload catalog. **`hl_notify_classes`** is the payload matrix (**40 rows**:
`heading_tpl` / `subline_tpl` / `with_message`) → two locked shells `notify_card` / `notify_card_sms`. Editing a
heading edits one row. 66 legacy `notify_*` rows quarantined (reversible). Migrations 0320 / 0321.

### 4b · The Blocks Engine — templates own every pixel (LOCKED 2026-07-05)

Templates own EVERY pixel of markup; code supplies ONLY data. Repeating structures are BLOCK rows
(`{{block:listing_card list=items as=listing limit=12}}` + `{{#ns.field}}…{{/ns.field}}`). `match-cards.ts` is
data-only. Migration `0319_blocks_engine`.

### 4c · Three families that must NOT collapse

**1. Agent notifications** collapse into the One-Card System. **2. Lead-facing intent templates** (door welcomes,
saved-search digests, tour/contact confirms, Myli `{{ai.*}}`) stay individual — one per intent, by design.
**3. Shared blocks** are the reused markup both embed. One engine, three shapes, never merged.

### 4d · Delivery — owner-only, provenance in the copy

- **Owner-only fan-out (LOCKED 2026-07-07)** — a lead notification goes to the lead's **owner only**; never
  collaborators, never AI agents. (Killed the Msale flood: 28 pings/6 people → 1.) `lib/notifications/create.ts`.
- **Provenance (LOCKED 2026-07-07)** — every card names **source** + **integration**: *"…requested a tour — 123
  Main St · from Zillow via Follow Up Boss."* From `hl_leads.source` + `hl_leads.platform` → `{{notify.source}}` /
  `{{notify.integration}}`, auto-appended to every card subline in one place. `lib/notifications/dispatch.ts`.
- **No rogue notifiers (LOCKED 2026-07-07)** — the tour/contact agent SMS that fired on `if(agent?.phone)` (ignoring
  the toggle + owner) is dead; routes through pref-gated `notifyLeadEvent`. Codebase swept clean.
- **Two-beat standard** — Beat 1 branded SYSTEM email instant; Beat 2 Myli ~2 min later (email + text). Every text
  is Myli (ONE NUMBER). **FUB self-heal** for call/email detail. **Voicemail + forwarding** under Settings → Phone.
